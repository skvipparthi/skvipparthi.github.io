Dr. Santosh Kumar Vipparthi, a senior member of IEEE, has 11+ years of experience including teaching and industry. Currently, he is an Assistant Professor in the Mehta Family School of Data Science and Artificial Intelligence, Indian Institute of Technology, Guwahati (IIT Guwahati). Before this, he served as an Assistant Professor in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at Malaviya National Institute of Technology (MNIT), Jaipur (An Institute of National Importance and one of the top NITs fully funded by the Ministry of Education, Government of India) (2013-2022). He received Ph.D. and MTech degrees with Honors in Systems Engineering from the Indian Institute of Technology (IIT Varanasi), BHU, India, in 2014, 2010 respectively, and a B.E. degree in Electrical and Electronics Engineering from Andhra University, Andhra Pradesh, India, in 2007. Dr. Vipparthi researches in the area of computer vision, deep learning, etc. He successfully supervised 03 research scholars (PhD’s) and is currently guiding 07 research scholars.
