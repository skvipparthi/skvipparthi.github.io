I am a Postdoctoral Research Fellow at National University of Singapore (NUS). I am working with Prof. Mohan Kankanhalli and Prof. Jussi Keppo in the N-CRiPT Lab. My current research is in data valuation, privacy and security in deep learning, and data privacy. I have extensive research experience in computer vision, machine learning, and deep learning.

I received my PhD in Computer Science from Malaviya National Institute of Technology, Jaipur, India. Previously, I received my bachelor and masters degree in Computer Science from BITS Pilani (2011) and Thapar University (2015).
